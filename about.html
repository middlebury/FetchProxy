About FetchProxy

FetchProxy is a small web service that transparently subscribes to RSS, iCal, and other data feeds so that clients don't have to wait on slow data sources.

If you are seeing requests on your webserver coming from a FetchProxy installation it is likely because the user of FetchProxy is incorperating your feed into their site and is using FetchProxy to speed access to your feed. A properly configured FetchProxy installation should only request each feed URL a few times per hour and should not overload your server.

Please lookup the IP address in your request logs to find administrator of the FetchProxy service that is hitting your site.

To download the FetchProxy application yourself, please see https://github.com/middlebury/FetchProxy
